Argentina came into the match against Ecuador with a streak of 8 consecutive victories, which began on November 22 against Brazil. In the Copa América, the Argentine team, world champion, had won its first three games without conceding goals against Canada, Chile and Peru. It seemed that they would continue this streak against Ecuador, but in the second added minute of the second half, Rodríguez equalized the score. Finally, under the direction of Lionel Scaloni, Argentina won in the quarterfinals on penalties. The Canadian team had a solid performance in the Copa América, adding 4 points in the group stage. Despite an initial 0-2 loss to Argentina, they achieved a crucial 1-0 victory over Peru and a 0-0 draw against Chile. In the quarterfinals, they tied 1-1 with Venezuela and advanced in the penalty shootout. It stands out that all of Canada's games were low-scoring, with only two goals scored in four games.
